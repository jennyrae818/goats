⚬ About Me ⚬

Hi! My name is Jenny Sacco.

Currently, I live in Tacoma, WA with my husband and two mini austrailian shepards.

I've worn a few different hats throughout my life: 9 years of Private Banking at Merrill Lynch, "little ripper" snowboarding instructor at Mount Bachelor, Assistant Teacher at a wonderful Montessori school in Bellevue, WA, and operations at a hostel in Bend, OR.

In 2017, I walked across the country of Spain over the span of 45 days. Link of "the way": The Camino De Santiago

Since 2019, I have proudly been serving my community as a Fire Dispatcher for Pierce County.

I own and operate a small house cleaning business named Forrest Green Cleaning, LLC.

I am best described as an organized, curious minded, creative, helper.

My goal is to help others through web development!
